# Agentic AI Foundation Social Media Governance \+ Guidelines

## Overview

The Linux Foundation leads the Agentic AI Foundation social media content strategy, access, and compliance with brand, legal, and communication policies. Content is published and approved by the Foundation staff to ensure consistency, accuracy, and alignment with project goals.

**Purpose**: These platforms are used to share Foundation-wide updates including releases, milestones, events, and initiatives relevant to the open source AI ecosystem.

**Tone**: Neutral, professional, inclusive, and clear. Built to serve a global AI \+ developer audience and reflect the integrity of the project.

**What We Post:**

* Official releases & large milestones  
* Foundation-led events, initiatives, and community calls  
* Ecosystem tools or content featured in official project work

**Community \+ Member Involvement:**  
We encourage community contributions\! If you have content related to an official release, event, or project effort, you can submit it for review. Final decisions and publishing remain with the Foundation team.

**End Goal:** To keep communication trusted, consistent, and community-aware while giving visibility to contributions that directly support the Foundation’s mission.

### How to Submit Content

There are two ways to submit content for social amplification, depending on the type of request.

* **For direct reposts**: Share in the [AAIF Discord Social Amplification Channel](https://discord.gg/xDBHRCkc)  
* **For organic content posted from AAIF channels**: Utilize our [Content Intake Form](https://form.asana.com/?k=Bq-lRvAXGzHQIXH_etPOxA&d=9283783873717)  
  * To keep things fair and scalable, we amplify organic content through structured programs rather than individual requests.

### Social Posts Baseline

* **Relevant to Agentic AI**: Keep posts, replies, and shares focused on the open source AI ecosystem, community events, learning resources, or related topics.  
* **Inclusive**: Please avoid exclusionary language, gatekeeping, or assumptions.  
* **No Unsolicited Promotions**: We welcome vendor-neutral blog posts, projects, or tools related to agentic AI in open source. Generic self-promo, advertisements, and spam will not be accepted.  
* **Give Credit**: Sharing code or ideas? Give proper attribution. Respect open source licenses and the effort behind contributions. Reposting individuals will be limited due the vast number of contributors.  
* **Platform-Specific Etiquette**: Each space has a specific vibe we strive for.  
  * **X**: Short, punchy, engaging – great for threads, release notes, or memes.  
  * **LinkedIn**: Professional tone – highlight case studies, team achievements, or thoughtful business and tech discussions.  
  * **Bluesky**: Casual and conversational – ask questions, share ideas, or be playful with updates.

## Usage Guidelines

### Reposting

We are glad to repost and like content on X, LinkedIn, and Bluesky. We welcome and support the broader open source AI community sharing their work across social platforms. This policy only governs what appears on our *official* AAIF channels.

**Here’s what we look for:**

* Tied to official project releases  
* Shared by core contributors or working groups to announce project changes  
* Connected to official Foundation events or initiatives

**We will not repost:**

* Community projects will be featured only when they explicitly showcase the implementation of AAIF standards (MCP, goose, etc.), helping to highlight the growth of our interoperable ecosystem.  
* Personal blogs, code tips, or videos  
* Vendor products or product announcements, consulting promotions, or sponsored content

**How to Be Featured on the Agentic AI Foundation Channels**

We’re always looking to highlight educational content that strengthens the project and informs the wider open source AI ecosystem. If you would like the AAIF Team to consider amplification for content tied to an Agentic AI Foundation release or event:

* Tag our official accounts on social media  
* Submit via Discord  
  * Please include a short description, relevant links, desired timing, and which Foundation initiative it supports.

### Timing

Please allow reasonable lead time for review. Last minute requests may not be accommodated. We will do our best to follow up with you if we have any questions or concerns, and if we can post your content within the timeframe requested.
